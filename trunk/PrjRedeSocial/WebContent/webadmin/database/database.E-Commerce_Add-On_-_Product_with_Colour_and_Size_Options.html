This add-on adds a template to use for products with colour and size options. Also adds a product with two colour options and three size options as a general product and two sub products with each of the colour options and 5 sub product with various size options.
Simply edit the products in the add-on to suit actual needs. Copy one of the sub products if more colour options are needed. Copy one of the sub products with sizes and colours to add more size options. Update the stock of the products with both size and colour and leave the stock of other product as zero. Enter your general product name in the Product name field below. Enter for example "Shoes". Enter a group name in the group name below. For example "Products with colours and sizes". All pages, templates, images and products associated with the import will be placed in groups with the chosen group name.
