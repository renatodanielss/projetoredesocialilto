News application for easy listing of news with RSS channel news story syndication of news, calendar selection of news stories and news story print template.
Simply add your news stories to the page group with the news group name you select below. For example if you type "News" in the News Group Name field below you will need to add your stories to the "News" page group. View summaries of your news stories with links to the full story. Summary pages are located in the page group with the name of the News Group Name you select below plus "Special". For example if you type "News" in the News Group Name field below your summary pages will be located in the "News Special" page group. For a summary page with a calendar select the "News Latest" page. For a summary page without a calendar select the "News" page.
