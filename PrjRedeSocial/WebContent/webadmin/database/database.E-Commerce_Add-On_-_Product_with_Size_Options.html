This add-on adds a template to use for products with size options. Also adds a product with two size options as a general product and two sub products with each of the size options.
Simply edit the products in the add-on to suit actual needs. Copy one of the sub products if more size options are needed. Update the stock of the sub products and leave the stock for the general product as zero. Enter your general product name in the Product name field below. Enter for example "Luxury Shirt". Enter a group name in the group name below. For example "Products with sizes". All pages, templates, images and products associated with the import will be placed in groups with the chosen group name.
